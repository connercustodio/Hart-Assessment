HART Assessment
Holistic Awareness & Regulation Test
by Conner Custodio

Many people carry symptoms — chronic fatigue, autoimmune conditions, anxiety, addiction, or simply a sense that something is off — without ever understanding why. The HART Assessment helps connect those dots.

This assessment was designed to give language to the things you have always felt but never been able to make sense of.

You will move through 8 areas of your life including physical load, nutrition, nervous system regulation, your formative story, lifestyle rhythms, relational environment, body history, and your roots and environment.

There are no right or wrong answers. Answer as honestly as you can.

This assessment is not where healing ends — it is where awareness begins. For the first time, you may have language for things you have always felt but never been able to explain.

Before You Begin

  • You must be 18 or older to continue
  • Some questions address sensitive topics including childhood experiences and sexual history
  • This is not a medical diagnosis or therapy — it is a structured coaching and research instrument
  • Your responses are private and used only for coaching and anonymous research purposes
  • If you are in crisis please contact a licensed mental health professional before continuing

Please note: this assessment does not save progress. If you close your browser you will need to start over. We recommend completing it in one sitting — it takes approximately 10–15 minutes.
